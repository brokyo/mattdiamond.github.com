About Web Projects Music Film
Matt Diamond is an award-winning composer based in the New York metropolitan area. He is an honors graduate of the Johns Hopkins University and the Peabody Conservatory, with degrees in neuroscience and computer music composition. He has composed the score for three independent films (Snake Hill, The BlueBelles, The Vanishing City), contributed ambient music to an art gallery exhibit (Courbet and the Modern Landscape: Summer), and composed music for various other commercial media works.
